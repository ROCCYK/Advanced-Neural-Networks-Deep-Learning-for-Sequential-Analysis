Week 2 Overview

This week: Introduction to PyTorch Tensors

Learning Outcomes

After completing this module, you will be able to demonstrate the following:

  1. Learning Outcome #1: Understand and explain the role of tensors as the building blocks of neural networks in PyTorch.
  2. Learning Outcome #2: Apply tensor operations to process data, including vector and matrix manipulations, to develop and train neural networks.
  3. Learning Outcome #3: Utilize PyTorch tensors for various applications, such as converting datasets and images into tensors for input into neural networks.

Assessment Reminders

Assignment # 1: Data preprocessing and manipulation in PyTorch, working with tensors.

  • Due: October 7
